What counts as a gas boiler?
A domestic gas boiler is a heating device that uses natural gas to generate hot water or steam for heating purposes within a residential property.
What counts as an oil boiler?
A domestic oil boiler is a heating device that uses oil as a fuel source to produce hot water or steam for heating purposes within a residential property.
